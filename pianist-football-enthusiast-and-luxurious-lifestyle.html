Pianist, Football Enthusiast, and Luxurious Lifestyle

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 4, 2024

Charlie Ocean Goldblum has been familiar with media attention since his birth. He is the son of American actor and musician Jeff Goldbum and the former gymnast Emilie Livingston. Jeff is an Oscar-nominated actor, who is well-known for his role in Jurrasic Park.

Unlike many other celebrity children, Goldblum is not hidden from the public eye. His loving parents keep sharing details of their eldest son on their social media. In this article, let's explore the details of Charlie's intriguing life.

Charlie Ocean Goldblum Age & Birthday

Jeff Goldblum and his wife, Emilie Livingston, welcomed their son, Charlie, on July 4, 2015. The celebrity pair were delighted and shared the birth of their son via Facebook. 

Goldblum first announced that they were waiting for a child in The Late Show with David Letterman Show. He said,

“It’s a boy. We picked out a name, as a matter of fact, If it stays — this is our current thinking — [it’ll be] Elliott Charlie.”

Jeff celebrated his 8th birthday on the independence day of 2023. Emilie wished him by sharing a glimpse of him having fun via Instagram.

First Camp At The Age Of 8

Shortly after celebrating his 8th birthday, Goldblum got to celebrate his first camp. Livingston shared it on Instagram on July 24, 2023, with pictures of her eldest son getting ready for the sleepaway camp. 

Further, the Canadian dancer revealed shedding tears realizing how her son has grown up. She wrote

I definitely shed some tears, feeling emotional that he’s already waving me goodbye with such maturity and taking it all in like a wise old soul!

Charlie's Parents Romantic Relationship 

Goldblum's parents have been in marital ties for almost a decade now. After a romantic proposal by Geena Davis's ex-husband, Jeff Goldblum in July 2014, the love birds tied the knot on November 8, 2014.

The former gymnast Emilie and Jeff first met in 2011 at the gym Equinox on Sunset Boulevard in West Hollywood. The American actor Jeff spotted her in the crowd of many. 

Gradually, the conversation started, and in no time, Charlie's parents began dating. Since then, the love between these romantic pairs has been intact. The married pair were also spotted on Taylor Swift's Eras Tour.

Charlie, A Protective Brother

Jeff and Emilie became a parent to yet another child, a son named River Joe Goldblum. He was born on April 7, 2017, which makes River two years younger than Charlie.

Charlie is a caring and protective brother. The Goldblum brothers are seen having fun together. It appears like they are each other best friends.

Despite the tight-knight bond, Charlie and River do have a sibling rivalry. The Canadian dancer Emilie keeps sharing pictures of her kids on social media. In one such post, Livingston disclosed that she was happy that the Goldblum's brother got to experience the life of Toronto briefly. 

Goldblum's Hobby Is To Play Piano

Charlie has inherited the musical talent of his father. Yes, the celebrity son is a pianist. While sharing a glimpse of Goldblum playing the piano, the Oscar-nominated actor Jeff wrote an emotional caption. 

Jeff also thanked his eldest son for the glorifying performance. Charlie learned to play piano under the tutelage of Kevin Anto. The celebrity son had his first recital and graduated in Piano Book 1.

Charlie Goldblum's Parents' Net Worth

Born into a wealthy family, Charlie lives a rich lifestyle. Thanks to the massive fortune of Livingston and Jeff Goldblum, the celebrity son has been privileged with many luxuries, such as foreign vacations and wearing fancy dresses, that many children dream of.

Speaking of his parents' wealth, Jeff Goldblum has a net worth of $40 Million. In addition to being an accomplished jazz pianist, Jeff is an actor with over 140 credits on his IMDb profile.

Charlie Goldblum resides in the Los Angeles mansion of his parents. The multi-million dollar mansion has been in the possession of Jeff since 1982. 

Goldblum's luxurious house is located in the Hollywood Hills. It includes three beds and three baths spread across a little over 3,000 square feet of living space.

Goldblum's Interest In Football

Charlie has found his love in football. He has been playing it since a very young age. He shares this love with his younger brother, River Joe Goldblum.

Emilie Livingston has often said that her boys are energy reserves. Even after spending time on the beach and surfing, they could still go to play football.

Looks like, Goldblum's favorite football team is PSG, where the son of Fayza and Wilfried Mbappe, Kylian Mbappe, plays. Jeff's eldest son was seen wearing a PSG jersey and posing in front of the PSG banner in a short clip his mother shared on his 8th birthday.

Is Charlie Good With Pets?

Yes, Charlie is good with pets as he grew up with them. The Goldblum has a pet dog named Woody Allen. It is a red-haired and well-groomed poodle. 

Goldblum prefers the company of animals. Besides dogs, he was also seen spending quality time with horses. He cleaned it and learned horse riding. 

Emilie Livingston said that their affection for horses was so much that they refused to return to the home for a while. The celebrity son was fascinated by the equestrian lifestyle.
