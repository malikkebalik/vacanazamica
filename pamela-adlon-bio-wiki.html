Who is Pamela Adlon?

Pamela Adlon is one of the most talented actresses in Hollywood. The gorgeous and attractive diva, Pamela Adlon is also a voice artist.

Presently, she is one of the prominent figures for her voice-over in the TV show, “Recess,” and “King of the Hill.” Additionally, her other TV projects are “Louie,” “Better Things,” and “Californication.”

Pamela Adlon: Birth, Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

Pamela Adlon was born in New York City in the state of New York. She was born on July 9, 1966. Her birth name is Pamela Fionna Segall. Her father’s name is Donald “Don” Maxwell Segall.

Similarly, the name of her mother is Marina L. Segall. Donald was a writer/producer. He mainly worked for TV shows. During her childhood, she lived in the Carnegie House building at 100 West 57th Street.

Although she was born in New York, she also lived for the most part of her early life in Los Angeles. She has English descent from her mother’s side.

From a young age, performing fascinated her. Pamela Adlon began acting and doing voice-over at the age of nine. For her education, she went to some schools. Later, she joined Sarah Lawrence College for a semester.

Pamela Adlon: Profession, Career

Pamela Adlon is in the acting industry since 1982. In 1982, she featured in a film, “Grease 2.” The next year in 1983, she joined the cast of a TV series, “The Facts of Life.” She started with some small roles.

Afterward, she featured in many TV and film projects like “Bad Manners,” “E/R,” “Night court,” and others. Then in 1989, she rebranded herself as a voice actress. She gave voice to ‘Ket’ in the film, “Kiki’s Delivery Service.”

Pamela Adlon was not a top demanded artist until 1997. In 1997, she signed with two TV series to give her voice to the characters. One was as ‘Ashley Spinelli’ in the Television show, “Recess.” And another was for giving voice to ‘Bobby Hill, Clark Peters, and Chane Wassonason’ in “King of the Hill.”

For her performance in ‘King of the Hill,’ she received a “Primetime Emmy Award.” Likewise, she built her career as a voice actress. Some of her other famous projects are “Californication,” and “Louie.”

Furthermore, she gave her voice to films like “Lucky 13,” “Tinker Bell,” “Dino Time,” and “Waiting for Woody.” In 2016, she featured in TV and film projects like, “First Girl I Loved,” “Mr. Pickles,” “Better Things,” and others. Recently in 2017, she was seen in the film, “I Love You, Daddy.” Her upcoming movie in 2018 is “Bumblebee.”

In 2016, Pamela Adlon also plays ‘Sam’ in the new TV series, “Better Things.” Moreover, she is the co-producer and writer of the show.

According to some websites, Pamela Adlon earns around $1.9 million per year. As of 2022, her estimated net worth is about $16 million.
