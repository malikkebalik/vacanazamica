Burt Bacharach Net Worth:- Today we are going to introduce Burt Bacharach along with his net worth and fortune. As you all know that Burt Bacharach was an American composer, songwriter, pianist, and record producer.

He composed hundreds of pop songs from the late 1950s. He was the 6-time Grammy Award winner and 3-time Academy Award winner. He passed away on 8 February 2023 at the age of 94. 

Burt Bacharach Net Worth

Burt Bacharach Net Worth: As an American composer, songwriter, record producer, pianist, and singer, Burt Bacharach made a huge net worth and that amount of his net worth is not a normal amount for most people. His net worth was around $180 million.

Burt Bacharach Cause of Death

Burt Bacharach passed away on 8 February 2023 on Wednesday at the age of 94 at his home in Los Angeles because of natural causes.

How The Iconic Music Man Burt Earned a Fortune

The Iconic Music Man Burt wrote more than 500 songs. When he died, at that time, he had a total of 6 NO. 1 hits to his name including The Guy’s In Love With You, Raindrops Keep Fallin’ On My Head, (They Long to Be) Close to You, Arthur’s Theme (The Best That You Can Do), That’s What Friends Are For, and On My Own.

Burt Bacharach Career

Burt was selected for the United States Army in 1950 where he served for two years. At that time his Army job in Germany, he met singer Vic Damone and following his discharge, he spent the 3 years as a pianist and conductor for Damone.

He made his appearances in tv shows and films such as An Evening with Marlene Dietrich, Austin Powers: International Man of Mystery, Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me, Austin Powers in Goldmember, Marlene Dietrich: Her Own Song, and Nip/Tuck, The Nanny, and Jake in Progress.

He released many albums titled Hit Maker!: Burt Bacharach Plays the Burt Bacharach Hits (1965), After the Fox (film soundtrack) (1966), Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid, Lost Horizon, Woman, Arthur, One Amazing Night, At This Time, and many more.

Burt Bacharach Wife

Burt Bacharach was married first to Paula Steward with whom he had married from 1953 to 1958.

His second wife’s name was Angie Dickinson with whom Burt had been married from 1965 to 1980.

His third wife’s name was Carole Bayer Sager with whom he had married from 1982 to 1991.

His fourth’s wife’s name was Jane Hansen with whom he had been married since 1993 until his death.

Burt Bacharach Children

Burt Bacharach had four children named Lea Nikki Bacharach, Cristopher Bacharach, Oliver Bacharach, and Raleigh Bacharach.

Burt Bacharach Family (Father, Mother)

Burt Freeman Bacharach was born on 12 May 1928 to Irma M. Bacharach (Mother) and Mark Bertram Bacharach (Father) in a Jewish family in Kansas City, Missouri, United States.

His father was a well-known syndicated newspaper columnist. His mother Irma was an amateur painter and songwriter.
