Joy Reid: Who is she?

Joy Reid is a well-known American cable television anchor, national reporter for MSNBC, liberal political pundit, and author. She rose to notoriety as the anchor of the weekly MSNBC morning show “AM Joy.” 

The New York Times labeled her a “heroine” of the resistance in 2018, citing her rise to prominence in left-wing political debate.

Reid’s book, “The Man Who Sold America: Trump and the Unravelling of the American Story,” was published in 2019 and digs into the political situation during Trump’s presidency.  

Particularly, MSNBC announced in July 2020 that Joy Reid would host “The ReidOut,” a new weeknight show based in Washington, taking over the 7 p.m. Eastern time slot vacated in March by long-time “Hardball” host Chris Matthews’ retirement, further solidifying her influence in political journalism and commentary.
