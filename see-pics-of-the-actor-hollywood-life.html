Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Talk about ‘Wow’ factor. Owen Wilson is a real gem. Check out this gallery of the amazing comedic actor’s best photos.

With his shaggy hair, trademark nose, and unmistakable spacey delivery, Owen Wilson has won his way into the hearts of moviegoers everywhere. Acting is a family business for Owen, whose brothers – Luke Wilson and Andrew Wilson – have appeared alongside Owen in a handful of films.

Owen is also known for his association with Wes Anderson, having worked with the director on films like Bottle Rocket, Rushmore, The Royal Tenenbaums, The Life Aquatic with Steve Zissou, The Darjeeling Limited, The Budapest Hotel, and The French Dispatch. Owen has also won over audiences in movies like Zoolander, Marley and Me, Wedding Crashers, and the Night at the Museum franchise.

In 2011, Owen and his then-girlfriend Jade Duell welcomed a son. He and Caroline Lindqvist welcomed a child together in 2014. Owen became a father for the third time in 2018 when ex-girlfriend Varunie Vongsvirates gave birth to a girl. For more fabulous photos of Owen over the years, take a look through the gallery here.
