Taylor Swift, her father Scott, brother Austin, and mother Andrea cheer on Travis Kelce during the C

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

The 34-year-old pop sensation enjoyed her vacation in Kansas City, where her boyfriend Travis Kelce’s Kansas City Chiefs played against the Raiders of Las Vegas, and Taylor invited her family to join her in the excitement.

The singer of “Anti-Hero” was accompanied by her parents, Scott Swift, 71, and Andrea, 65, on Monday as she entered Arrowhead Stadium. Austin Swift, her brother, also showed up there, dressing as Santa Claus to go unnoticed.

Austin’s fiancée, Sydney Ness, was also there during the game; she was spotted with Taylor in the box, donning a hoodie that a nearby boutique had given her.

Swift had her brother walking by her as they entered the stadium. Her parents trailed in their wake.

How are Travis and Taylor’s families getting along?

While she was in Philadelphia, Travis’ mother, Donna Kelce, previously told PEOPLE that she would be “spending time with my grandkids as we cheer on their dad,” her oldest son Jason, who performs for the Eagles of Philadelphia.

Just over a week had passed since Scott and his daughter had gone to see the Chiefs play at Gillette Stadium in Foxborough, Massachusetts, on December 17.

Travis Kelce claims that Taylor Swift’s dad Scott was “rocking” in a Kansas City Chiefs sweatshirt during the Patriots game.

In one particular game, Taylor sported a grey Chiefs crewneck and a white knit cap imprinted with Travis’ jersey number, “87,” while Travis wore a red team sweatshirt.

When the NFL star went to Buenos Aires, Argentina, last month to see one of Taylor Swift’s Eras Tour shows, the singer’s father had his first encounter with Travis.

On the football players’ podcast, New Heights with Jason and Travis Kelce, after the two first met, Jason, 36, made fun of Scott for giving up his lifetime support of the Philadelphia Eagles in favor of his daughter’s new boyfriend.

Travis gave the tight end a Chiefs lanyard to watch his daughter perform in Buenos Aires, which made him quip that he “got ’em all over here to the good side, baby”—a reference to the Swift family’s previous support of Jason’s team.

“Scott, what are we doing?” The center stated, “Just one by one, getting all the good ones to come on over,” while Travis went on. “You’re gonna let this man’s devilishly good looks and relationship with your daughter sway you from a lifetime of fandom, Scott?” Jason asked, pushing back against his brother. 

When did Travis Kelce start dating Taylor Swift?

In 2023, Kelce started dating singer-songwriter Taylor Swift. The widely reported relationship has had a significant effect on NFL viewership; tickets for the Chiefs home game and Kelce’s jersey sales increased, and the Chiefs vs. Bears game, which Swift attended, had the highest television viewership of the weekend.

The NFL and NBC made use of their partnership to market upcoming games and create social media content.

Swift witnessed the Chiefs-Jets game, which had an average audience of 27 million viewers, making it the most viewed Sunday-night TV program since Super Bowl LVII.
