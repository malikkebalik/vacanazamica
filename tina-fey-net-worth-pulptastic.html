What is Tina Fey’s Net Worth and Salary?

Tina Fey is a renowned American actress, comedian, writer, and producer with a net worth of $75 million. She is most notably associated with the popular comedy sketch show, Saturday Night Live.

However, Fey has achieved success in various other fields as well, and is hailed as one of the most humorous women in the industry. She has received several awards during her illustrious career.

Fey is the creator, star, executive producer, and writer of the show “30 Rock”, which aired 139 episodes over seven seasons. In addition, she is a highly sought after spokesperson and awards show host.

Tina Fey’s Early Life and Education

Tina Fey was born on May 18th, 1970 in Pennsylvania to parents who encouraged her love of comedy at a young age. She watched shows like Saturday Night Live and Second City Television, and actress Catherine O’Hara became an early role model.

As a middle school student, Tina was already interested in a career in comedy. In high school, she joined the drama club and honed her comedic writing skills with a satirical column for the school newspaper.

Tina Fey went on to attend the University of Virginia, where she studied to be a playwright and an actor. She was awarded a Pettway Prize for her scholarly achievements and graduated in 1992 with a Bachelor of Arts degree in drama.

Tina Fey’s Career in Comedy

Tina Fey started her career in comedy in Chicago after studying with the Second City comedy troupe and performing with her future husband, Jeff Richmond. She began sending scripts for comedy sketches to Saturday Night Live and was eventually hired as a writer. Fey’s big break came in 1999 when she became the show’s first female head writer.

Fey made her film debut in the comedy Martin & Orloff in 2002 and continued to host the Weekend Update on Saturday Night Live alongside Amy Poehler. She wrote and starred in the hit comedy Mean Girls in 2004 and left Saturday Night Live in 2006 to create her own show, 30 Rock, which won multiple awards including an Emmy and a Golden Globe.

In 2015, Fey created the show Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt, which moved from NBC to Netflix partway through its run and ended in 2019. Fey also appeared in a range of other TV shows with guest roles and is an accomplished stage actress. Her autobiography Bossypants was a successful venture, and she has appeared in commercials for American Express, Allstate, and Garnier.

Marriage and Children

In 1994, Tina Fey began dating Jeff Richmond, a fellow member of the Second City troupe and an improvisational pianist. They eventually tied the knot in 2001 and went on to have two children together.

Tina Fey’s NYC Real Estate Investments

In 2005, Tina and Jeff purchased a New York City apartment for $1.9 million. The following year, they bought a smaller condo nearby for $550,000, which Tina now uses as her office.

In 2009, the couple purchased a $3.4 million apartment in the Upper West Side of New York City. The apartment boasts four bedrooms, a dressing room, a library, a living room, a dining room, a kitchen, a butler’s pantry, four full bathrooms, a half bath, and elevator access. Despite its luxurious features, they reportedly secured it at a reduced price of $3.4 million, instead of the asking price of $4.1 million.
