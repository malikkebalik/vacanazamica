Hottest Womens Denim Cut Off Shorts in 2023 Hollywood Life Reviews Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

The top women’s denim cut-off shorts in 2023 make a great addition to any wardrobe for the summer. You can dress them up or wear them to lounge around on the beach. They are also an essential vacation item. Whatever your style, a pair of denim cut-off shorts are versatile enough to match it.

Denim cut-off shorts are available in different lengths and styles. Choosing the best type for your style and body type might seem difficult. However, there are several things you can consider before you purchase denim cut-off shorts to ensure you get the best product in terms of comfort and style.

Continue reading to see what we suggest.

Things to Consider Before Purchasing Women’s Denim Cut Off Shorts

Denim washes

Denim wash describes the effect applied to denim to create a certain look. Different denim washes include:

Stonewash makes a new fabric look a little more worn and beaten. The process uses stones and makes the fabric feel softer.

  • Enzyme wash

Enzyme wash also makes the fabric look more faded, and feel softer.

  • Acid wash/bleach wash

Acid wash is the act of partially washing denim with bleach. This can be done in certain patches to make the shorts look more faded in different areas. Alternatively, the shorts could be fully bleached making them look extremely faded.

  • Mill wash or rinse wash

This technique creates the darkest least faded color in denim.

Distressing

Distressed denim is ripped or frayed to varying levels. Most denim shorts are distressed to some degree. This creates a cool and more youthful look.

Color

Denim shorts are available in every color under the rainbow. However, the most popular colors are blue or black. For this reason, if you want denim shorts in a different color to blue or black, you should begin your search with a specific color.

Waist-length

Denim cut-off shorts can sit at different parts of the body near or on the waist. Where the shorts sit can be described by the following phrases:

This means the shorts will rest on the hips. This look suits tall people best.

Mid-rise denim shorts sit on the belly button.

High-rise denim shorts will sit somewhere between the smallest part of your waist and below the ribs. These shorts will elongate your legs and are perfect for shorter people.

Inseam length

You can purchase denim shorts that show some of your bottom, denim shorts that sit just above the knee, and every length between. Choose a length that you feel comfortable in, and is weather appropriate.

Side slit

Some women’s cut-off denim shorts have a side slit. The side slit adds to the style of the shorts and allows you to move a little more freely in them.

Stretchy

Similarly, stretchy denim shorts will allow you to move more freely. Sometimes shorts can fit perfectly when you stand but may feel too tight when you sit. Purchasing denim shorts with a little stretch will ensure you remain comfortable.

Closure type

Most denim cut-off shorts have a zip and button closure. However, you can also purchase denim cut-off shorts which use buttons alone to fasten. Alternatively, you may want to look for a pair of denim shorts with an elasticated waist.

Hem

The hem of the shorts can be secure and smooth. However, raw hemlines are in trend right now. Raw hemlines can range in how much they are frayed. The hemline you choose is down to your personal preference.

Pockets

Pockets are extremely useful. Denim shorts usually have at least two pockets but may have none and as many as five or six. Some pockets are fake and are just placed on the shorts to create a certain look.

Denim skorts

Skorts are a mix between shorts and a skirt. This is a great option if you love the look of a denim skirt, but you want to create a little more modesty. They also bring a touch of individuality to an outfit.

Denim short dungarees

Denim short dungarees are another great option that brings a twist to both the denim shorts look and the dungaree look.

Customer reviews

Before you purchase denim shorts, be sure to read the product’s customer reviews. This will give you a better idea about the short’s durability, as well as how true to size the product is.

Women’s Denim Cut-Off Shorts Price Range

Women’s denim cut-off shorts can cost from as little as $7 and as much as $90 for a pair with a designer label.
