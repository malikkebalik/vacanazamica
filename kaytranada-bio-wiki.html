Who is Kaytranada?

Kaytranada, renowned by his stage name Louis Kevin Celestin, and Kaytra is a well-known Haitian Canadian DJ and record producer.

Kevin came into the limelight after releasing a series of mixtapes, remixes, and original music projects.

In 2013, Celestin started receiving huge recognition, and the next year, he signed a deal with XL Recordings.
