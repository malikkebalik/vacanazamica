Nick Sandow is an American actor, producer, writer, and director who is best known for portraying roles such as Joe Caputo in Orange Is the New Black, Garza in Meadowland, Joe Lombardo in Third Watch, and Murray Clarke in Clarice. He has also appeared in a variety of other TV shows and films such as Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, Boardwalk Empire, CSI: Miami, Blue Bloods, Six Degrees, How to Make It in America, Stella’s Last Weekend, The Cold Lands, A Brooklyn State of Mind, On the Run, and many more. His theatre credits include plays like Halfway Home, Baptism By Fire, and Henry Flamethrow.

    Nick Sandow Facts

  • He grew up in an Italian neighborhood in The Bronx.
  • He was raised by a single mother.
  • His father died of a heart attack when he was just 9 years old.
  • In April 2004, he acted in the Michael Imperioli play Baptism By Fire at Studio Dante in New York City.
  • In June 2005, he directed Henry Flamethrowa, a play by John Belluso at Studio Dante, New York City.
  • He has also done several different credible roles in shows like Boardwalk Empire, Blue Bloods, How to Make It in America, Third Watch, and NYPD Blue.
  • He wrote and directed a mob film, The Wannabe which premiered at the Tribeca Film Festival in 2015.
  • Scorsese’s films that influenced him in the making of The Wannabe are Italian films like Salvatore Giuliano and Mafioso and the American thriller Mean Streets.
