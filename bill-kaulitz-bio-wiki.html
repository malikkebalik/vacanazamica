Who is Bill Kaulitz?

Bill Kaulitz is a German singer, songwriter, model, fashion designer, and voice actor.

He along with his twin brother Tom Kaulitz are members of the rock band Tokio Hotel.

Bill Kaulitz: Bio, Age(33), Parents, Nationality

Bill Kaulitz was born on September 1, 1989, in Leipzig, Germany. His birth name is Bill Kaulitz-Trumper. He was born to his father Jorg Kaulitz and his mother Simone Kaulitz.

He has an identical twin brother – Tom Kaulitz who is 10 minutes older than him. Their parents divorced when he was 7 years old.

After that, his mother went on to date Gordon Trumper, guitarist of the German rock band Fatun. After 12 years of dating his mother married Gordon on 1 August 2009.

Bill holds German citizenship and his ethnicity is also German.

Bill Kaulitz: Professional Life and Career

Bill Kaulitz was interested in singing from a young age, which was noticed by Trümper and helped the Kaulitz twins to start their own band. The twin began writing music at the age of 7.

The Kaulitz twins started live in Magdeburg performing at the age of 10 but the band lacked a drummer and bass player.

Eventually, when Bill Kaulitz was 12 years old, he and Tom Kaulitz met bassist Georg Listing (then 14), and drummer Gustav Schafer (then 13). They named the band Devilish but later renamed it Tokio Hotel.

Bill then auditioned on Star Search, a reality TV show, which got them recognized. After that, Tokio Hotel signed with Universal Music Group’s Interscope Records and released their debut LP Schrei in 2005.

The single, Durch den Monsun, from the LP, reached number 1 in Germany followed by the second album Zimmer 483 in February 2007.

Then, they released their debut English album, titled Scream in Europe (mid-2007) and in Western countries (mid-2008).

Similarly, they released Humanoid (October 2009), Kings of Suburbia (214), and Dream Machine (2017).

Tokio Hotel has also released compilation albums Best Of TH (2010), Darkside of the Sun (2011), 2 live albums, 3 EPs, and has had around 7 tours.

Moreover, Bill Kaulitz has lent his voice to Arthur in Arthur und die Minimoys. In addition, he started to model and worked as a jury member in the 10th season of Deutschland Sucht den Superstar in 2010.

As a fashion designer, he has designed clothes for Magdeburg -Los Angeles, shortly known as MDLA.
