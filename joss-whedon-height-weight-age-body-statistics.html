Joss Whedon is an American filmmaker, composer, and comic book writer who is the founder of Mutant Enemy Productions and co-founder of Bellwether Pictures. He is best known for being the creator of several popular TV series like Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Angel, Firefly, Dr. Horrible’s Sing-Along Blog, Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D., and The Nevers. He has also created a variety of other TV shows and films like Toy Story, Alien Resurrection, Serenity, Much Ado About Nothing, The Cabin in the Woods, Avengers: Age of Ultron, Justice League, and many more. During the course of his career, Joss has been honored with numerous awards such as the Eisner Award, Emmy Award, and Saturn Award.

Joss Whedon has dated

  • Kai Cole (1995-2016) – In 1995, Joss Whedon married Kai Cole, who is an architect and the co-founder of Bellwether Pictures. In December 2002, she gave birth to their son, Arden, whose godparents are actress Alyson Hannigan and husband Alexis Denisof. Two years later, Kai gave birth to a baby girl, Squire. However, they decided to separate in 2012, and their divorce was finalized in 2016. She wrote a scathing letter, in 2017, accusing Joss of being a hypocrite, who pretended to believe in feminist ideals to promote his career. She alleged that he had multiple affairs, including emotional ones, with co-workers, actresses, friends, and fans during the course of their marriage. She also claimed that Joss himself had confessed of his adulterous behavior in a letter he wrote while their marriage was falling apart.

    Joss Whedon Facts

  • When he was creating Buffy’s character for the popular TV series, he intended to create an alternative feminist icon.
  • The producers of the 2000 movie X-Men asked him to revise the script of the movie. Joss, however, felt that the whole script had to be rewritten. In the end, his rewritten script was rejected as they only wanted him to add a couple of jokes.
  • He has revealed in his interviews that the X-Men Character Kitty Pryde (AKA Shadowcat) served as one of the major inspirations for the character of Buffy.
  • He is credited as the driving force behind making Thanos, the main villain of the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
  • He has been honored twice (in 2006 and 2013) by the Equality Now organization, which works to promote and protect the human rights of women all over the world. Additionally, he has also been appointed to the advisory board of the Equality Now Organization.
  • In February 2021, Buffy the Vampire Slayer actress, Charisma Carpenter accused him of on-set abuse and misconduct.
