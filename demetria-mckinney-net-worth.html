Demetria Dyan McKinney has an estimated net worth of $3 Million


Demetria Dyan McKinney Wiki Biography

Demetria Dyan McKinney was born on the 27th August 1980, in Albuquerque, New Mexico USA. She gained recognition as an actress and singer, notably for her role in the TV comedy sitcom “House of Payne” (2006-2012), created by director and producer Tyler Perry. Her career in the entertainment industry began in 2000.

Wondering about Demetria McKinney’s wealth? It is estimated that her total net worth is $3 million, primarily earned through her acting career. In addition to acting, McKinney has also ventured into singing, releasing several singles that have contributed to her net worth.

Demetria Mckinney Net Worth $3 Million

McKinney’s childhood was marked by frequent relocations, as her father served in the US Military. She attended Fort Walton Beach High School in Florida before embarking on her professional career in 2004. Despite this, McKinney had been honing her talents from an early age, participating in church choirs, and further developing her skills through vocal scholarships, theater, and dance studies.

Her breakout role was as Kim Brown in the play “Meet the Browns,” written by Tyler Perry, with whom McKinney continued to collaborate in subsequent years. She appeared in several of Perry’s productions, such as “What’s Done in the Dark” and “Why Did I Get Married,” alongside her most renowned portrayal as Janine Shelton-Payne in the sitcom “House of Payne”. This role garnered her a Best Performance in a Comedy nomination and significantly bolstered her net worth. Recently, McKinney appeared in the 7th season of “The Real Housewives of Atlanta”.

Her on-screen success propelled McKinney to expand her acting repertoire to the big screen, featuring in films like “Daddy’s Little Girls,” another collaboration with Tyler Perry, and “In the Meantime,” among others, substantially augmenting her net worth.

In addition to her television and film endeavors, McKinney has dabbled in theater, having performed on Broadway in the musicals “I Dream” (2010) and “Dreamgirls” (2012).

Furthermore, McKinney delved into the R&B music scene in 2011, establishing herself as a singer. Singles such as “Get Yo..Ish,” “Take Away This Love,” and “Work With me” have served as additional sources of her wealth. Throughout her music career, she has collaborated with artists like Anthony David, Musiq Soulchild, and R. Kelly. McKinney released her first EP titled “Officially Yours,” featuring music videos for the songs “Trade it All,” “100,” and “Work With Me”.

Regarding her personal life and interests, McKinney is reportedly in a relationship with TV producer Roger M. Bobb, whom she met while on the “House of Payne” set. However, the couple remained private about their relationship until the show concluded due to Tyler Perry’s fondness for McKinney. Despite this, Demetria has expressed that music remains her primary passion, and the EP is just the beginning, with more of her talent yet to be unveiled.
