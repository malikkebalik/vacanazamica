GloRilla Clears Up Rumor She Went To Prison For Stealing Cereal

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 6, 2024

GloRilla has clarified the story going around that she was once arrested and did three years behind bars over stolen boxes of cereal.

The CMG rapper responded to the rumors in a series of tweets on Wednesday (April 5), where she emphatically set the record straight and tied the narrative to society being so gullible these days.

“You hoes really sat y’all ass up there & believed I went to jail for 3 years for stealing cereal & dats why you hoes hearts be in y’all ass today because y’all believe whatever a n-gga tell you,” she wrote.

“& dats da reason you N-ggas be taking care of kids dats not y’all’s till they 12 because you believe whatever a ho tell you!”

& dats da reason you Niggas be taking care of kids dats not y’all’s till they 12 because you believe whatever a ho tell you https://t.co/2q8GAsMA3Y

— GloRilla 🦍 (@GloTheofficial) April 5, 2023

Last May, Big Glo sat down for an interview with Funny Marco’s Cornbread TV and was quizzed about rumors of her stealing cereal boxes from K-Mart and subsequently being sentenced to three years in prison.

The “Tomorrow 2” rapper confirmed that she went to jail for the crime, but said it with a smirk on her face as the show is known to be satirical at times.

“So, they actually put that on there. Wow, they put my business on there. Yeah, I did,” she said after being asked about the rumor. “I don’t like the fact that you dug that up. Yes, I did. He didn’t make that up.”

GloRilla had even more words for the haters on Twitter this week as she brushed them off and explained she doesn’t care about being liked.

“I never gave a single fuck about a ho/n-gga not liking me & a mf bet not lemme find out Dey don’t like me cause now im finna work yo nerves & do everything in my power to make you hate me ho,” Glo added.
