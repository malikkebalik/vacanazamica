Craig Robinson’s Age & Early Life

Craig Robinson was born on October 25, 1971, in Chicago, Illinois, to parents Fraser C. Robinson III and Marian Shields Robinson. He has a brother named Chris Rob. He will be 52 years old in 2024. Robinson is of American descent and of mixed (African-American) ancestry. His zodiac sign is Scorpio. For his education, he attended Whitney M. Young Magnet High School, followed by earning a bachelor’s degree from Illinois State University and a master’s degree from Saint Xavier University. He also worked as a music teacher at Horace Mann Elementary School.

Career

Before gaining fame as an actor, Robinson began his career as a comedian in Chicago. Furthermore, he pursued acting at The Second City, an institution that has produced talents such as Tina Fey and Amy Poehler. He has made appearances on various shows including Arrested Development, Reno 911!, and Eastbound & Down. Additionally, he lent his voice to the character Cookie in the animated Shrek film series.

During his early years as a comedian, he collaborated with ‘Jerry Minor’ as part of the musical duo L. Witherspoon & Chucky. They made appearances on Comedy Central, Real Time with Bill Maher, and Jimmy Kimmel Live. Moreover, Robinson is a member of the band Nasty Delicious and has been featured on Last Comic Standing and in an episode of The Office. He also contributed to the soundtracks of Hot Tub Time Machine with performances of “Jesse’s Girl” and “Let’s Get It Started.”
