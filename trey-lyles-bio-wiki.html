Who is Trey Lyles?

Trey Lyles is a Canadian-American professional basketball player who presently plays for the Utah Jazz of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He had represented the University of Kentucky in basketball at the state and national levels. He received the Indiana Mr. Basketball title.

How many siblings does Trey Lyles have?

Talking about his early life, Trey Lyles was born on November 5, 1995, to a Canadian mother, Jasenka Lyles, and an American father, Thomas Lyles. His father also used to play basketball and is a musician. The family stayed at her mother’s native place, Saskatoon, Canada.

When Trey was 7 years old, the family shifted to Indianapolis, Indiana. Here he used to practice basketball day and night with his father. This helped him since at his Arsenal Tech High School he soon was the best basketball player. He made his school powerful in this game at the state level.

He was a powerful defender. He joined the University of Kentucky and there too he was amongst the top players.

Trey Lyles: Career, Salary($ 2.24 m), and Net Worth($ 4.4 m)

Trey Lyles’s career soared when he was at the University of Kentucky with the country’s best basketball players. He also played for Canada in 2012 and took Canada’s junior squad to the 6th position at the 2013 under-19 world championships.

He was picked up in round 1 by Utah Jazz with the 12th overall pick in the 2015 NBA draft. His play statistics at Utah Jazz have also been fantastic and he vows to increase it further with his hard work and sincerity.

His earnings in 2016 were $ 2.24 million. As of 2023, he is estimated to have a net worth of $ 4.4 million.
