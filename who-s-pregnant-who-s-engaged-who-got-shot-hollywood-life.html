Who's Pregnant! Who's Engaged! Who Got SHOT! Hollywood Life

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 12, 2024

An otherwise so-so season finale was totally saved by the last 10 minutes full of crazy twists!

SPOILER ALERT! If you haven’t seen the May 17 season finale of One Tree Hill, STOP READING! I had the pleasure of live-tweeting the madness for HollywoodLife.com, and I’d still appreciate someone helping me pick my jaw up off the floor. There’s no way this show isn’t going to get picked up for at least half a season in the fall — there’s WAY too much story left to tell.

Clay & Quinn get shot!

I knew we hadn’t seen the last of crazy Katie (Amanda Schull!) In the episode’s final minutes, she appeared in Clay (Robert Buckley) and Quinn’s (Shantel VanSanten) bedroom with a gun! Without saying a word she shoots them both in the chest.

Julian finally proposes to Brooke!

Fresh off the success of his movie’s premiere at the big Utah film festival, Julian (Austin Nichols) takes girlfriend Brooke (Sophia Bush) for a romantic stroll in the woods, where he slips her a ring attached to a VERY big question… and of course, Brooke says yes!

A baby girl for Nathan & Haley?

Jamie Scott (Jackson Brundage) might be getting a little sister next season! In one of the final scenes of last night’s episode, a finally happy Haley (Bethany Joy Galeotti) tells husband Nathan (James Lafferty) that she’s pregnant, and she thinks it’s a girl!

New love triangle: Alex, Chase & Mia!

Now that Alex (Jana Kramer) has decided to stay in Tree Hill, she’s setting her sights on newly single Chase (Stephen Colletti.) But when he starts getting texts from his ex-girlfriend Mia (Kate Voegele) he realizes he has to make a choice.

Skills & Mouth are BFFs again.

Apparently Skills (Antwon Tanner) and Mouth (Lee Norris) are done fighting over Ms. Lauren (Allison Munn.) They’re now free to fight over who has the more ridiculous nickname.

No sign of Dan or Millicent.

We may have seen the last of Dan (Paul Johanssen), but there’s definitely more to Millicent’s (Lisa Goldstein) story. Why didn’t she go to Utah with everyone? At least she works for Julian’s fiancé! What did Chase do for the movie?

What a crazy way to end an already ridiculous season!

— Andy Swift
