'Sister, Sister:' Update on Hit Show's Cast 20 Years after it Ended

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 18, 2024

The hit television sitcom "Sister, Sister" premiered on April 1, 1994, and concluded after airing six seasons on May 23, 1999. Find out what the cast has been up to this past 20 years.

The series discussed the tale of two identical twins who were separated during childbirth and adopted into different families but later reunited as preteens.

As indicated by Digital Spy, the main cast have all remained in the media industry. You can catch up with them all below.

TIA MOWRY

Tia played as Tia Landry. She's the mature and responsible sister on the show. Since the "Sister, Sister" wrapped up, she has acted along with her sister Tamera Mowry in "The Hot Chicks."

But as of late she has more become a TV chef, cooking on her own show, "Tia Mowry at Home." She has written her own cookbook too.

The actress, who is a married mother-of-two, is also currently starring in the "Family Reunion," a Netflix comedy.

TAMERA MOWRY

Tamera played Tamera Campbell on "Sister, Sister." Her character in the show was the careless sister.

After the beloved show ended, she starred in several films, including "Seventeen Again" and "The Hot Chicks."

Like her sister, Tamera is also married and has two children. At present, she co-hosts a talk show called "The Real."

TIM REID

Tim Reid at Stone Mountain Golf Club on August 24, 2019 in Stone Mountain, Georgia | Photo: Getty Images

Tim played Tamera's adoptive father Ray Campbell. He's the patriarch of the show too.

Since then he has starred in "That 70's Show" and the recent TV adaption of "Limitless."

He's the founder and president of a non-profit organization called Legacy Media Institute.

JACKEE HARRY

Jackee played as Lisa Landry in "Sister, Sister." Because of her excellent performance on the show, she won an Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy series.

Since then, the actress has starred in several television shows, including "2 Broke Girls," "Instant Mom" and "Everybody Hates Chris."

Jackee likewise routinely performs in theatre productions, recently performing in the US stage play "The Cleanup Woman."

RONREACO LEE

RonReaco played the role of Tia's boyfriend, Tyreke Scott. He was cast in smaller roles after "Sister, Sister" until he was picked to star as Jamal Woodson in the romantic comedy show, "Let's Stay Together" from 2011-2014.

After this, he appeared in the drama series "Survivor's Remorse."

DEON RICHMOND

Performing the role of Tamera's boyfriend Jordan Bennett, Deon has had smaller roles in "Scream 3" and "Not Another Teen Movie" after "Sister, Sister" ended.

He additionally had several TV roles, including the sitcom "Teachers" in 2006.

MARQUES HOUSTON

Marques played the role of Roger Evans, the neighbor of Tia and Tamera on "Sister, Sister." Because of his mom's battle with lymphoma, he left the show before it ended.

Since the show, he starred in several TV and film roles, including "You Got Served" and "Fat Albert." He has also pursued a music career.

In 2018, a revival of "Sister, Sister" was confirmed and is right now in progress.
