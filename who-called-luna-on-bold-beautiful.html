Who Called Luna on Bold &amp; Beautiful?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, September 12, 2024

Color us intrigued. When Lisa Yamada made her Bold & Beautiful debut as Luna on September 13, the Forrester Creations intern received a call from a woman warning her to stay away from L.A.’s most fashionable family. But who? Obviously, the mystery woman has a history with Eric’s kinfolk, and one that she doesn’t want to see Luna repeat. But who is she?

Macy Alexander

Since Madame X is wary of the Forresters, she would have to be someone who tangled with them and came out on the losing side, right? First person who comes to mind is the OG Sally Spectra’s late daughter, Macy Alexander, who could never make a marriage to Thorne stick. Bobbie Eakes recently hinted that her character might not be as dead as we all believed. And since it’s been two decades since Deacon’s wife was on the canvas, Brooke’s longtime rival could easily have had a new relationship that left her with a grown daughter like Luna.

Morgan DeWitt

Also long gone from the canvas is Morgan DeWitt, the designer who was coerced by Stephanie Forrester into aborting her and Ridge’s child. Her interactions with the Forresters only exacerbated her mental illness — to the point that she sicked a python on Clarke Garrison and terrorized the family while dressed as a clown. When last seen, she was bound for (a lot) more therapy. If she’s got her head screwed on straight now, she’d certainly want daughter Luna not to follow in her footsteps and let the Forresters throw her off-kilter.

April Knight

A long shot is Amber Moore’s twin sister, April Knight. In 2003, the singer briefly hung her guitar strap in L.A. — and no doubt noticed how chaotic her surprise sibling’s life among the Forresters was. If April had a kid — in other words, Luna — she would probably recommend that she not get mixed up in the drama that had made Aunt Amber’s existence a roller coaster of baby swaps and blackmail plots.

Michael Lai

Another option is Michael Lai, who was pals with Jessica Forrester in the 1990s and lost Grant Chambers to Brooke. She’d certainly have gotten close enough to the Forresters and the tidal wave of drama that follows them around to want to ensure that her offspring steer clear. But, as we’ve already seen, Luna is about to plunge head-first into the hurricane that is life among the Forresters.

Beverly

This formerly homeless former Forrester intern would have a major reason to warn any friend or loved one away from the designing dynasty. First, queen bee Stephanie was all about her. Then… nada. Stephanie got Beverly a job, then pretty much forgot that she existed. Even decades later, that’s gotta sting!

Li Finnegan

Finally, maybe we’re all wrong, and it’s not a past character who is warning Luna away from the Forresters but a present one: in other words, Li. Finn was on track for happily ever after as the most abtastic doctor in the US when he fell for Steffy and got embroiled in her family’s nonsense. So if Luna is Li’s niece, goddaughter, something like that, she’d definitely want her not to repeat Finn’s mistake. Thing is, wouldn’t we recognize Li’s voice on the phone?
