The Rise of The Superhumans!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

An Amazon Prime program called “Gen V” is now airing and is set in the same world as “The Boys.” It centers on a generation of young people who received early doses of Compound V and are currently enrolled in Godolkin University, an institution designed to turn these Superhumans into Superheroes.

But you already know what a large company dealing with superheroes is if you’re familiar with The Boys. The show centers on a group of brave young people who dared to challenge the status quo. Their first goal is to learn the truth about their buddy Luke’s brother and free him from the cell he has been held in since faking his death many years before.

Here’s everything you need to know about Gen V Season 1 Episode 6, including its release date, time, and where you can watch this.

Gen V Episode 6 Storyline

After a party, the gang wakes up at Dusty’s place to begin the episode, but no one can recall what happened that night. They accuse Rufus and his abilities. When Sam locates everyone, he discovers that Vought has erased their memories and that they have forgotten about him.
