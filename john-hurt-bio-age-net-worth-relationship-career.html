John Vincent Hurt was born on January 22, 1940, in Chesterfield, Derbyshire, England, into the family of mathematician Arnold Herbert Hurt and engineer Phyllis Massey.

Out of the three children, John was the youngest. He went to Lincoln School after attending St. Michael’s Preparatory School in Otford, Kent.

He spent two years as a student at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art. Hurt passed away on January 25, 2017, at the age of 77, at his home in Cromer, Norfolk.

Despite receiving a diagnosis of early-stage pancreatic cancer in 2015 and entering a state of remission, he died of natural causes.
