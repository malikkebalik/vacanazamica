Who Is Tomer Capon?

Tomer Capon, a former military man who participated at the dangerous war area including Israeli disengagement from Gaza and the Lebanon War (2006) as a battalion combat soldier and later a squad commander from the Israeli Defence Forces, is an Israeli actor and model.

Growing up in Israel, Capon lived most of his childhood in Holon and Rishon LeZion. Similar to the majority population of Israel, Capon was raised in a wealthy Jewish family where his parents owned their own business.

Capon, however, carried interests to become a soldier but that later changed when he developed a sudden change of his passion for acting. Ultimately, Tomer then joined the Yoram Levinstein’s acting school where he studied for a year. He was 26 at the time.

Furthermore, Tomer Capon worked alongside his fellow co-stars; Jordana Lajoie, Karen Fukuhara, Nicola Correia Damude, Haley Joel Osment, Laila Robins, and Brendan Beiser.
