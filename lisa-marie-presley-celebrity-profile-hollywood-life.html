Lisa Marie Presley Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, July 28, 2024

Bio:
Lisa Marie Presley (born on Feb. 1, 1968 in Memphis, TN) is an American actress, song writer, and recording artist. She was famously the daughter of the late Elvis Presley and actress Priscilla Presley, but she built her own career with several rock, country, blues and folk albums. She has been married four times, including to Michael Jackson and actor Nicolas Cage. She is a famous Scientologist, and her faith has lead her to pursue several humanitarian efforts including fighting against ADHD medication and the Literacy, Education and Ability Program.

Personal Life:
Lisa Marie Presley grew up in Memphis, TN, with her parents Elvis and Priscilla. When they divorced, she stayed with her mother. She is now the sole heir to the Elvis estate. In 1988, she married musician Danny Keough. They had two children, Danielle Riley and Benjamin, before divorcing in 1994. The duo stayed friends, and just twenty days later she married Michael Jackson. She supported him through his child molestation case, but they divorced in 1996. She married Nic Cage in 2002, ad he filed for divorce just 108 days later. Lisa married Michael Lockwood in 2006, and gave birth to twins Harper and Finley.
