I-Brock O'Hurn Biography

Brock O'Hurn ngokungangabazeki uphakathi kosaziwayo abasemfashinini, abade kakhulu, futhi abanesiphiwo abavela e-United States of America. Ungumqeqeshi ozimele owaziwayo e-Los Angeles. Ngaphandle kwentshisekelo yakhe ekuqeqesheni ukuqina, uthanda nokulingisa kumamuvi.

Ukhombise ikhono lakhe elimangalisayo lokulingisa emafilimini. Abantu abaningi abavela ezindaweni ezahlukahlukene zomhlaba bazi u-Brock njengosaziwayo be-Instagram.

Ubehlekwa ngezinye izingane ngenxa yobude bakhe. Ezinye izingane esikoleni zazivame ukumgcona; ngenxa yalokho, wazizwa enamahloni ngokuba mude ngokweqile esemncane. Kwakumele alalele amazwi amabi avela kubantu ngenxa yomzimba wakhe omncane.

Ngemva kokulinyazwa iningi lezingane ezingontanga yakhe, wakhetha ukuzakhela umzimba wakhe ube nesisindo esifanele. Waqala izikhathi zokuzivocavoca futhi wathuthukisa uhlelo lwakhe lokudla lokwakha imisipha. UBrock waqala ukuphakamisa izinsimbi esikoleni samabanga aphezulu.

U-Brock wazabalaza ukuthuthukisa umzimba wakhe ukuze ube ngobude nokunciphisa isisindo samanje. Wenze amanye amadoda abe nomona ngaye ngenxa yokubukeka kwakhe okuhle. Le ndoda isingomunye wosaziwayo be-Instagram abashisa izikhotha emphakathini waseMelika wezokuxhumana.

Izinwele zakhe ezinsundu zilokhu zigeleza, okunezela ukukhanga okukhulu ebuntwini bakhe obuthakazelayo. Abalandeli bakhe bayamthanda bebona ihembe elinezinwele eziboshwe. Inani lezithandwa zika-Brock likhula ngesivinini esisheshayo, futhi kubonakala sengathi maduze uzoba yingxenye ebalulekile yebhizinisi laseHollywood.

Ngemva kokuqeda isikole esikoleni esiphakeme, u-Brock waqala ukusebenza enkampanini kaMalume wakhe isikhathi esifushane. Le nhlangano ibandakanye ukumiswa kwezinhlelo zokupholisa nokushisisa.

Waqhubeka nokusebenza ebhizinisini likaMalume wakhe ngaphambi kokuba athole omunye umsebenzi e-True Religion nase-Abercrombie nase-Fitch. Ubeyisisebenzi esizikhandlayo, lapho ebesebenza khona ngokuzikhandla ukuze aziphilise. Akekho noyedwa kubaqashi bakhe owake wakhononda ngokuphambene nesimilo sakhe.
