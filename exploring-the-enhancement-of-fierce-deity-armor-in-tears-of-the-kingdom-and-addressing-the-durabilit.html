Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 22, 2024

Delving into the Upgrade Process of Fierce Deity Armor in Tears Of The Kingdom

The remarkable Fierce Deity Armor set comprises the Fierce Deity Armor, Mask, and Boots, each possessing its own strength in their basic state. Enhancing these items through upgrades not only amplifies their capabilities but also unlocks the coveted Armor Set bonus.

If your goal is to boost the Fierce Deity armor set in Tears of the Kingdom, understanding the associated costs is crucial. To aid you in this endeavor, here are detailed insights into the upgrade requirements:

Enhancing the Fierce Deity Armor

  • Level 1: 15 Hinox Toe Nails and 1 Dragon Scale each from Naydra, Dinraal, Farosh
  • Level 2: 15 Hinox Teeth and 3 Dragon Claws (from Naydra, Dinraal, Farosh)
  • Level 3: 6 Hinox Guts and 3 Dragon Fangs (from Naydra, Dinraal, Farosh)
  • Level 4: 6 Lynel Guts and 3 Dragon Horns (from Naydra, Dinraal, Farosh)

Enhancing the Fierce Deity Mask

  • Level 1: 15 Hinox Toe Nails and 1 Dragon Scale each from Naydra, Dinraal, Farosh
  • Level 2: 15 Hinox Teeth and 3 Dragon Claws (from Naydra, Dinraal, Farosh)
  • Level 3: 6 Hinox Guts and 3 Dragon Fangs (from Naydra, Dinraal, Farosh)
  • Level 4: 6 Lynel Guts and 3 Dragon Horns (from Naydra, Dinraal, Farosh)

Enhancing the Fierce Deity Boots

  • Level 1: 15 Hinox Toe Nails and 1 Dragon Scale each from Naydra, Dinraal, Farosh
  • Level 2: 15 Hinox Teeth and 3 Dragon Claws (from Naydra, Dinraal, Farosh)
  • Level 3: 6 Hinox Guts and 3 Dragon Fangs (from Naydra, Dinraal, Farosh)
  • Level 4: 6 Lynel Guts and 3 Dragon Horns (from Naydra, Dinraal, Farosh)

When collecting Dragon materials, ensure you obtain them separately from Naydra, Dinraal, and Farosh. Additionally, rupee costs for each upgrade level are as follows: 10 rupees for Level 1, 50 rupees for Level 2, 200 rupees for Level 3, and 500 rupees for Level 4.

Understanding the Durability of Fierce Deity Sword

The Fierce Deity Sword serves as a valuable asset during the initial and middle phases of the game, especially when paired with formidable materials. Boasting a base damage of 38, it outshines the Master Sword right from the beginning. However, its durability is a point of concern as it is prone to breaking without external support.

Similar to other weapons in the game, prolonged usage may lead to the eventual breakage of the Fierce Deity Sword. Despite this drawback, it showcases commendable durability and a substantial attack power of 38. If the sword succumbs to wear and tear, do not fret as it can be repurchased from a merchant statue later in the game for 160 poes.

Unveiling the TOTK Fierce Deity Armor Set

The formidable Fierce Deity Armor set makes a grand entrance in Tears of the Kingdom, allowing players to obtain it within the game without reliance on an amiibo. Legend has it that this legendary set belonged to a heroic figure in a world threatened by a falling moon. An homage to Majora’s Mask, wearing this armor significantly enhances offensive capabilities. Discover the steps to acquire the coveted Fierce Deity Armor Set in TOTK, consisting of three essential pieces:

  • Fierce Deity Armor – Body Gear (Base Defense: 9)
  • Fierce Deity Mask – Head Gear (Base Defense: 9)
  • Fierce Deity Boots – Leg Gear (Base Defense: 9)
