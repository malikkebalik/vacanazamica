DJ Kaskade is an American DJ, record producer, and remixer who was voted “America’s Best DJ” by DJ Times in 2011 and 2013. His discography consists of albums and tracks like It’s You, It’s Me, The Calm, Fire & Ice, Love Mysterious, Automatic, Atmosphere, Kaskade Christmas, Dynasty, Feeling the Night (ft. Becky Jean Williams), In the Moment, Strobelite Seduction, Cold as Stone (ft. Charlotte Lawrence), Room for Happiness (ft. Skylar Grey), What I Say, All That You Give (ft. Mindy Gledhill), In This Life, Sorry, Lessons in Love (ft. Neon Trees), Angel on My Shoulder, With You (with Meghan Trainor), Gonna Make It, When I’m With You (with Colette), Closer, Where Did You Go, and Hypnotized.
