Kai Cenat shows off his love for New York as he reveals massive back tattoo

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

NEW YORK CITY, NEW YORK: Popular streamer Kai Cenat shared a post on his Instagram story, unveiling a new full back tattoo featuring the AMP logo and honoring his upbringing in New York City.

Cenat's identity is inextricably bound to his New York birthplace. The streamer is a native New Yorker who is passionate about the city.

What does Kai Cenat's new tattoo look like?

Kai Cenat's new full-back tattoo has the AMP symbol on his lower back. 'Any Means Possible' is written over his shoulder blades, alluding to his AMP content house.

But the rest of the tattoo pays homage to New York. Both the Statue of Liberty and a New York subway car are clearly visible. In addition, there's a big street sign that says The Bronx and has two Tims hanging from it.

New York City hasn't always returned the favor to the YouTuber. Cenat was taken into custody earlier this year after sparking a minor altercation in Union Square.

Cenat likes getting tattoos and frequently posts sneak peeks of them on social media. The precise number of tattoos on Cenat's body is unclear. However, there are enough, as his arms and chest are covered in amazing and lifelike ink drawings.

How did Kai Cenat become an Internet sensation?

It's amazing how Kai Cenat went from being an Instagram user, who shared funny cartoons, to becoming a social media sensation.

A burgeoning fan base was soon drawn to his distinct brand of humor, which prompted him to expand across several platforms, including Twitch and YouTube.

As a prominent participant in the YouTube partnership AMP, Cenat now has over a million followers on Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube, in addition to over 310 million views.

His creative ability and growing notoriety point to a fruitful and successful professional journey in the digital industry.
