DX News Bits: Beanie Sigel, Suge Knight

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

Following last years talks of Beanie Sigel possibly joining 50 Cent’s G-Unit, Sigel has revealed that 50 Cent was only using him for publicity for his album, Before I Self Destruct.

Sigel explained to The Source that he wasn’t upset about 50 Cent’s actions and that he was in fact using 50 Cent as well.

“If 50 Cent is using Beans to do what he’s going to do, that’s cool,” Beanie Sigel told The Source. “But at the same time, Beans is using 50 Cent to do what I need to be done. So at the end of the day, we use each other, but we don’t misuse each other.”

AD LOADING...

Beanie Sigel is currently at work wrapping up his next project, Roc Boys, which will also feature rapper Freeway. Roc Boys is expected to be released on February 23.

In other Hip Hop news, court documents have revealed that former Death Row CEO Suge Knight is living quite modestly compared to just years ago.

Knight is currently being sued by Tammy Renell Hawkins, the mother of his four-year-old child, for child support. Court documents, which were attained by TMZ, state that Knight is only making around $1,300 a month.

If child support is granted to the child’s mother, Knight will have to dish out around $300 a month.
