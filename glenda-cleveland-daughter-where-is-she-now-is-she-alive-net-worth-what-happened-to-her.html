Glenda Cleveland Daughter, Where Is She Now? Is She Alive? Net Worth, What Happened To Her?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, September 13, 2024

Glenda Cleveland was an American woman and the neighbor of serial killer Jeffrey Dahmer who was the first one to report him to the police when he was on a killing spear during the period between 1978 to 1991, killing 17 boys and men and committing physical assault on them.

He was arrested in July 1991. He was found guilty by the court in 1992 and was convicted of multiple charges of first-degree murder. However, he was killed by a fellow inmate while he was serving his sentence in the prison.

His life story is being narrated through the web series “Monster: The Jeffrey Dahmer Story” which premiered on Netflix on 21st September 2022, and in which, Emmy award winner Niecy Nash is playing the character of Glenda Cleveland.

Glenda Cleveland Daughter Sandra Smith

There is no clarity if Cleveland was married or not, but she surely had a daughter named Sandra Smith. Sandra is a nurse in Milwaukee. 

She allegedly saw a bleeding and naked boy running away from Jeffrey’s house, thus reporting it to her mother. 

She used to live with her daughter in the Oxford Apartments in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, and was one of the neighbors of serial killer Jeffrey Dahmer. 

Where Is Glenda Cleveland Now?

Glenda Cleveland is not in this world now. She died on 24 December 2010. She used to live in the next building of Jeffrey Dahmer’s house. 

She was one of her parents’ 9 children and grew up on a farm in Mississippi. She used to work of data entry. 

Is Glenda Cleveland Alive?

No, Glenda Cleveland is not alive. She passed away on 24 December 2010 at the age of 26.

Glenda Cleveland Net Worth

Glenda Cleveland’s net worth, before her death, was not known.

What Happened To Glenda Cleveland?

Glenda was born in the year 1954 in the United States. She was 56 years old when she died on 24th December 2010 when she was alone in her apartment due to her heart disease and high blood pressure.
