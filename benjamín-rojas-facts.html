Benjamín Rojas: The Multi-talented Argentine Actor and Singer

A Singer’s Bio

Benjamín Rojas is a multi-talented Argentine actor and singer, born on April 16th, 1985 in La Plata, Argentina. He began his career in the entertainment industry at the age of 11, as part of the kid’s music group “Mambrú.” Throughout his teen years, he grew into a successful actor, starring in popular shows such as “Chiquititas” and “Rebelde Way.” However, music remains a significant part of his artistic output, and in 2003, he released his first solo album “Benjamín Rojas” with Sony BMG.

Age and Relationships

Benjamín Rojas is now 36 years old, and he has had a few noteworthy relationships throughout his life. In the early 2000s, he dated Argentine actress Camila Bordonaba, whom he worked with on “Chiquititas” and “Rebelde Way.” The couple’s on-screen chemistry translated into real-life love, but they ended things in 2004. Rojas was also linked to actress María Eugenia Suárez, whom he met while filming the TV series “Casi Ángeles.” Still, they broke up after a while.

Children

Benjamín Rojas is a proud father of a son, whom he welcomed with his former partner, Martina Sánchez Acosta, in 2017. The couple keeps their lives private, and little is known about Rojas’ son.

Height

Benjamín Rojas stands at a respectable 5’10” (1.78m).

Career

Rojas’ acting career began at a young age, acting in various commercials and TV shows. However, his big break came when he was cast as the lead in the hit teen drama series “Rebelde Way.” The show and its cast became a massive sensation in Latin America, and Rojas quickly became a part of the region’s pop culture landscape. After “Rebelde Way,” Rojas appeared in several other TV programs, such as “Floricienta” and “Los Simuladores,” cementing his status as one of Argentina’s leading actors.

Aside from his acting, Rojas has been committed to music throughout his career. After his success with “Mambrú,” he went on to record several solo albums, including “Benjamín Rojas,” “Uno Mismo,” and “Eléctrico.” He has also contributed to several soundtracks for the TV shows he has acted in, such as “Rebelde Way” and “Casi Ángeles.”

More recently, Rojas has explored other avenues within the entertainment industry. He has acted in various films, such as “El Pájaro,” “Los Perros,” and “Despido Procedente.” In addition, he has taken on hosting duties, presenting shows like “MTV Uruguay Music Week” and “Los 10+”.

    The Verdict

    Benjamín Rojas is a multi-talented artist who has made a considerable impact on Argentina’s entertainment industry.
