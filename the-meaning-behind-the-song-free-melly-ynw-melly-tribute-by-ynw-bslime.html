The Meaning Behind The Song: Free Melly (YNW Melly Tribute) by YNW BSlime

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 19, 2024
TitleFree Melly (YNW Melly Tribute)
ArtistYNW BSlime
Writer/ComposerYung Shad & YNW BSlime
AlbumNot specified
Release DateAugust 6, 2019
GenreNot specified
ProducerNot specified

YNW Melly’s little brother, YNW BSlime, released a song titled “Free Melly (YNW Melly Tribute)” in honor of his incarcerated brother. This heartfelt tribute is a plea for Melly’s release, expressing the loyalty and love YNW BSlime feels towards his older brother.

The lyrics of the song convey a strong message of support and devotion to YNW Melly. In the chorus, YNW BSlime raps, “My brother was 17, was stacking that cheddar. Follow him, like a green light he made it better. Oh, free Melly, yeah, I’m screaming that forever. ‘Til it’s backwards, yeah, I got you here whatever.” These lines reflect the admiration YNW BSlime holds for his brother’s success and the desire to see him free from his current situation.

YNW BSlime also addresses the negativity surrounding YNW Melly’s case and how people are using his brother’s situation for personal gain. He expresses his frustration with those who are only looking for clout and attention, emphasizing his unwavering support for YNW Melly regardless of the negative talk surrounding him.

As listeners, we can feel the deep bond between the two brothers. YNW BSlime’s lyrics reflect the pride he has for his brother’s talent, as he raps, “He turned nothing into something. From the G now he rides. Won’t cut us up like a spiral.” These lines highlight YNW Melly’s rise to fame and success, and how he has become an inspiration to his younger brother.

Personally, this song touches me deeply because it reminds me of the love and loyalty I have for my own siblings. The bond between siblings is often indescribable, and YNW BSlime captures that essence in his tribute to YNW Melly. It serves as a reminder of the importance of family and the lengths we will go to support and stand up for each other.

The release of “Free Melly (YNW Melly Tribute)” demonstrates the power of music as a way to express emotions and connect with others. YNW BSlime’s heartfelt lyrics provide a glimpse into his relationship with YNW Melly and the impact his brother has had on his life.

While we wait for YNW Melly’s legal situation to be resolved, “Free Melly (YNW Melly Tribute)” stands as a testament to the loyalty and love between the two brothers. It serves as a reminder that even in difficult times, the bond between siblings remains unbreakable.
