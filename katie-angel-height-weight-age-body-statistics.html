Katie Angel is an American singer, songwriter, actress, dancer, social media star, and YouTube personality who is known for uploading videos on her self-titled YouTube channel. She started her journey as a YouTuber on May 19, 2015, and her videos usually revolve around makeup tutorials, health, challenges, life hacks, pranks, song covers, fitness routines, dance, and other relevant and engaging things. Apart from that, Katie Angel also runs a collaborative channel, Katie OnTheRoad, with her husband, ItsOsoTV, on which they upload content based on their daily lives. Katie OnTheRoad has been a huge success and has also accumulated a tremendous fan base with more than 5.5 million subscribers and more than 350 million views on the videos. She has also branched out as a singer and has released a couple of singles, such as Mal De La Cabeza and ME DA IGUAL, which have been received positively by the audience. Katie Angel has also amassed a huge social media fan base by constantly sharing her pictures and videos with her fans and has garnered more than 350k followers on Twitter, more than 6 million followers on Instagram, more than 900k followers on Facebook, and more than 12 million subscribers on her eponymous YouTube channel.
