Anastasia Radzinskaya

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

Anastasia Radzinskaya, who is both Russian and American and goes by the name “Like Nastya” online, is a child YouTuber, vlogger, TikToker, Instagrammer, and social media star.

What is the Net Worth of Anastasia Radzinskaya? Salary, Earnings

Anastasia Radzinskaya has a net worth of $21 million as of 2022. She made her net worth by putting up ads and working with brands on her social media accounts.

She is known for her YouTube channels like Like Nastya, Like Nastya Vlog, and Like Nastya Show. Anastasia is one of the YouTubers who has the most fans around the world.

She is one of the youngest YouTube stars in the world, and she became very famous very quickly. She and her parents run different YouTube channels for kids that are watched regularly by a very large number of people.

Where was Anastasia Radzinskaya born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Education

Anastasia Radzinskaya is 8 years old as of 2022. She was born on January 27, 2014. Radzinskaya was born and raised in Krasnodar Krai, in southern Russia, in a family from the upper middle class. She is known to have both Russian and American citizenship and to follow the Christian faith.

Anastasia Radzinskaya’s parents are Sergey and Anna Radzinskaya. Sergey Radzinskaya was Anastasia’s father, and he ran a construction business.

Anastasia Radzinskaya’s mother’s name is Anna Radzinskaya she had a bridal salon. Together with their daughter, they have started a lot of YouTube channels for kids.

She is getting her basic education at a High School in Florida, United States, right now. A few years after she was born, her family moved to Florida.

After she was born, she was diagnosed with cerebral palsy. Even her doctor thought she would never be able to talk, but thanks to her treatment, she got better and was able to speak again.
