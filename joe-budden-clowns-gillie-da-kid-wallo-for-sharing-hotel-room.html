Joe Budden Clowns Gillie Da Kid &amp; Wallo For Sharing Hotel Room

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 21, 2024

Joe Budden has clowned Gillie Da Kid and Wallo about the Million Dollaz Worth of Game co-hosts allegedly sharing a hotel room on the road.

Fans were eagerly awaiting Budden’s reaction to the cousins’ hotel room split and the former Slaughterhouse rapper brought it up to continue his petty war of words with Gillie over the weekend.

AD LOADING...

“I know n-ggas keep waiting on me to kill Gillie and Wallo for sharing a hotel room,” Budden began to seemingly check their pockets, which puzzled his co-hosts. “They posted it and they be in the same room.”

Budden continued: “Nah, they both in the bed. There’s nothing wrong. N-ggas can’t even say facts no more without it looking shady as hell.”
