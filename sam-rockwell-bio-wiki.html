Sam Rockwell Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 14, 2024

Who is Sam Rockwell?

Sam Rockwell is a popular American actor. He is extremely prominent for his leading role in the film, ‘Choke’ (2008), ‘Moon’ (2009), ‘Seven Psychopaths’ (2012), and ‘Mr. Right’ (2015), ‘Iron Man 2’ (2010), ‘Cowboys & Aliens’ (2011), and ‘The Way, Way Back (2013), etc.

Sam Rockwell: Birth Facts, Family, Childhood

Sam was born on November 5, 1968, in Daly City, California, U.S.A. His nationality is American and belongs to Irish and English ethnicity.

He was born to Pete Rockwell(father), an actor, and Penny Hess (mother). His parents divorced when he was only five years old. Since then he was raised by his father.

He used to spend his summer holidays in New York along with his mother. Being a son of an actor he was highly encouraged by his father to secure his career in the acting field.

In which school did Sam Rockwell attend?

Concerning his educational background, he attended the San Francisco School of the Arts but didn’t complete his graduation and dropped out of the school.

Later on, he attended Outward Bond-style alternative high school and received his high school diploma.

Sam Rockwell: Professional Life, Career, Awards

Sam made his debut in a 1989 film titled “Clownhouse” and appeared as ‘Randy’. He didn’t get so much recognition because of his minor role in the film.

Later on, he also appeared in other several movies including “Last Exit to Brooklyn” as ‘Al’, “Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles” as ‘Head Thug’, “Strictly Business” as ‘Gary’ etc.

In the year 1997, he appeared in a movie “Lawn Dogs” as ‘Trent’ for which he won two awards: ‘Montreal World Film Festival Award’ and ‘Catalonian International Film Festival Award’ in the category of best actor.

In the year 2002, he appeared in the film “Confessions of a Dangerous Mind” for which he won the ‘Berlin International Film Festival Award’ in the category of best actor.

Awards

He has also won several awards including ‘Newport Beach Film Festival’ for the film “A Single Shot”, ‘Saturn Awards for the film “Moon”, ‘Hollywood Film Awards for the film “Moon” and also nominated for more than 15 awards.

How much is the net worth of Sam Rockwell?

He has a net worth of $30 Million but his salary is not revealed yet.

Sam Rockwell: Rumors, Controversy

Once there was a rumor regarding Sam’s relation with Bibb. Fans and critics were telling that Sam and Bibb got separated. Later on, the news was found to be false and they announced that they were still living happily as a couple.

Body Measurement

Sam has a height of 5 feet 9 inches. His body weighs 72kg. He has light brown hair and brown eyes. Furthermore, his shoe size is 10(US).
